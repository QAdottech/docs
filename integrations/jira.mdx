## Jira

Jira is a software tool developed by Atlassian used for project management, issue tracking, and team collaboration. Initially designed as a bug-tracking tool, it has evolved into a versatile platform used by teams across industries to plan, track, and manage their work.

## Jira Integration

Connecting QA.tech to your Jira account is simple. Follow these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Jira">
    Go to [**Settings →
    Connections**](https://app.qa.tech/current-project/settings/organization/connections),
    and add the Jira integration. Follow the OAuth flow to grant access and set
    your preferred permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the Project">
    Navigate to [**Settings →
    Integrations**](https://app.qa.tech/current-project/settings/integrations?focus=jira).
    Select the Jira board and list where you want created issues to appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export an Issue">
    In the Issues section, select an issue and click the "Send to Jira" button
    to export it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
